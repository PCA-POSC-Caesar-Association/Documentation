---
title: Dependency Review and Status Updates
---

This guide explains the platform features that support reviewer decisions.

## Why dependencies matter

A review outcome can affect other content. The platform therefore exposes dependency information before a reviewer records approval or rejection.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the submitted review item.
2. Start the approval or rejection action.
3. Inspect the dependency information shown by the platform.
4. Confirm that the impact is understood.
5. Provide the required justification.
6. Record the status update.

## What the platform checks

The review implementation includes support for:

- viewing submitted items
- seeing approval dependencies
- seeing rejection dependencies
- updating status once the required review information is in place

## Why this matters

This keeps review actions more traceable and helps ensure that decisions are not made blindly without understanding impact.
